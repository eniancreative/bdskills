Frequently Asked Questions

Addressing Common Business Concerns

(01)
How can BD Skills help my business stay compliant?
  • BD Skills provides expert guidance on navigating complex regulations such as Employment Equity (EE), B-BBEE, and skills development. We ensure your business remains compliant while minimizing disruption to your operations.
(02)
How can workforce development be aligned with business goals?
  • We help you create and implement effective Employment Equity Plans and Workplace Skills Plans that are both compliant and strategically beneficial, ensuring your workforce is aligned with your business objectives.
(03)
How does BD Skills help in SETA funding opportunities?
  • Our consultants develop tailored Workplace Skills Plans (WSPs) that are designed to maximize your access to available SETA funding, helping you offset training costs and support workforce development.
(04)
How can BD Skills improve my B-BBEE score?
  • We offer tailored B-BBEE consulting services that focus on improving your B-BBEE score through strategic planning and implementation, without compromising your other business goals.
(05)
What are the benefits of stakeholder engagement?
  • Effective stakeholder engagement and an inclusive culture are key to long-term success. BD Skills helps you foster these elements, ensuring compliance with employment equity requirements and enhancing overall business performance.
